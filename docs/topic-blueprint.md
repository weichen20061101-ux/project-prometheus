# Project Prometheus Topic Blueprint

## Global Rules

- Learner stage: some basic exposure, but concepts are still unstable.
- Assessment mix: 80% objective questions, 20% open response.
- Preferred quiz size: 5 questions per set.
- Passing threshold: 80%.
- Visual support:
  - Style: exam explainer cards + realistic context illustrations.
  - Placement: above the question block.

## Analyst Track

- Outcome:
  - Build the ability to study financial statements and supporting company materials for fundamental analysis.
- Tone:
  - Teacher-led foundation building.
- Question modes:
  - Single choice
  - Multi-select
  - Boolean
- Materials:
  - Structured notes
  - Official external references
  - Worked example before the learner answers
- Material depth:
  - Detailed, close to full teaching notes.
- External source priority:
  - Company official materials
  - Official government / department information
  - Major news websites
- Multi-select target:
  - Usually 2 to 3 correct answers
- Avoid:
  - Obvious answers

## Finance English

- Outcome:
  - Read finance English and annual-report sentences with basic confidence.
- Tone:
  - Teacher-led foundation building.
- Question modes:
  - Primarily single choice
  - Supported by boolean checks
- Materials:
  - Worked example before answering
- Material depth:
  - Medium, enough to support answering
- Language:
  - Mixed English and Chinese
- Priority order:
  - Sentence understanding
  - Finance sentence patterns
  - Vocabulary understanding
  - Annual-report reading
- Example walkthrough:
  - Step-by-step
- Avoid:
  - Obvious answers
